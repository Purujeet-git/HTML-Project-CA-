Services Detail

At Calm Corner, we are dedicated to supporting students on their mental health journey. Our website serves as a vital resource, offering a variety of features tailored to your needs. Explore educational resources, including articles, infographics, and videos on essential topics like stress management and self-care. Utilize our interactive self-assessment tools to better understand your mental health, and access online therapy and counseling for confidential support from licensed professionals. We provide crisis resources, community support forums, and peer mentoring programs to foster connection and encouragement. Discover mindfulness and wellness tools, stay informed about upcoming events and workshops, and take advantage of our anonymous help option for a safe space to seek guidance. With a comprehensive directory of on-campus and community services, Calm Corner empowers you to understand and improve your mental health in a supportive and welcoming environment.

Mental Counselling Method

Psychoanalytic Therapy

Psychoanalytical therapy, rooted in the work of Sigmund Freud, offers a deep exploration of the unconscious mind and its influence on our thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. This therapeutic approach can be particularly beneficial for students navigating the complexities of academic life and personal development.

Mindful Based Therapy

At Calm Corner, we believe in the power of mindfulness to enhance mental well-being. Mindfulness-Based Therapy focuses on cultivating present-moment awareness, helping students manage stress and anxiety effectively.

Humanistic Therapy

Humanistic Therapy emphasizes personal growth and self-discovery, offering a compassionate and empathetic approach to mental health. At Calm Corner, we create a safe space for students to explore their feelings and experiences.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a highly effective, evidence-based approach that empowers students to understand and reshape their thought patterns. At Calm Corner, we use CBT to help you tackle the challenges of student life with confidence.
